Elderly Care — Silver, Gold & Diamond Packages

Himalayan White Cloud supports seniors to live safely and comfortably at home by arranging personalised, day-to-day services such as grocery supply, cleaning, safety checks, medicine support, hospital appointments and travel assistance. Each package (Silver, Gold, Diamond) includes the same service options — the difference is the number of services selected and the level of coordination.

What We Can Arrange

Families can choose the services needed. These are the core areas of support we coordinate:

Grocery & Essentials

Groceries, household items and essentials delivered or arranged as required.

Safety & Security Checks

Home visits, phone check-ins and safety monitoring depending on availability.

Bill Payments

Support with routine bill payments and reminders for due dates.

Cleaning & Home Maintenance

Light cleaning, tidying, and basic maintenance coordination.

Medicine Services

Medicine pickup, reminders and communication with pharmacies.

Hospital / Clinic Assistance

Booking appointments, reminders and accompaniment when possible.

Travel Assistance

Transport support for appointments, shopping or social visits.

Our Packages

Silver Package – Essential Support

Designed for seniors who need light assistance. Choose any 2 services.

  • Basic coordination
  • Monthly schedule review
  • Ideal for simple needs or tight budgets

Gold Package – Guided Care

For families needing more regular help. Choose any 4 services.

  • Priority coordination
  • Regular schedule management
  • More frequent family communication

Diamond Package – Premium Care Coordination

For high-needs situations requiring close involvement. Choose any 8 services.

  • High-touch coordination
  • Proactive follow-ups & issue tracking
  • Ideal for overseas families or complex needs

Pricing & Add-Ons

Base package price begins at 25,000 (local currency) per month for all three tiers. Monthly cost = base price + selected add-on services (fully itemised). Packages are flexible and upgradeable anytime.
