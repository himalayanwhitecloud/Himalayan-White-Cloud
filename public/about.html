About Himalayan White Cloud Service

Himalayan White Cloud Service is dedicated to supporting key moments in life from education and global mobility, to caring for loved ones and expanding trade. We combine practical coordination with personalised guidance so that you can make confident decisions and move forward with clarity.

We are a team of "brothers from different mothers" united not by the pursuit of wealth, but by the real, profound satisfaction that comes from serving others. Our mission is rooted in the belief that genuine success is measured by the positive impact we make. We are driven by a passion for fairness and real justice by ensuring access, opportunity and transparency in every service we provide.

Who We Are

We are a service-focused team that connects clients with trusted partners for study abroad, immigration and consultation, elderly care coordination and Trade X import & export support. Our work is built on listening carefully, explaining options in simple language and staying involved from the first question to the final step.

Whether you are planning an overseas education, finding the right elderly care package or exploring trade opportunities, our goal is to make each journey smoother, safer and more transparent.

What We Do

  • Guide students and families through study abroad planning and applications.
  • Support elderly care arrangements with Silver, Gold and Diamond packages.
  • Coordinate product sourcing, logistics and documentation through Trade X.
  • Provide clear, realistic advice based on current policies and requirements.

Every service is customised to your situation so there is no “one size fits all.”

Share your plans for study, care or trade, and we will prepare a clear, structured response with options that fit your situation.
