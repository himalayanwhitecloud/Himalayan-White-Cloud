Our 7-Step Experts Process

We guide you from initial counselling to visa lodgement and pre-departure, ensuring a safe and confident journey.

  1. English Test & Language Support – Advise on IELTS, TOEFL, PTE requirements and connect you with preparation resources.
  2. Free Consultation & Profile Check – Discuss goals, budget, academic background and preferred cities. Verify eligibility for study and financial planning.
  3. Course & Institute Selection – Shortlist suitable programmes and universities, explain entry requirements, fees and future pathways.
  4. Application Preparation & Submission – Prepare documents such as transcripts, CV, SOP and recommendation letters. Submit applications and follow up with institutions.
  5. Accept Offer & CoE – Review offers, pay required fees, obtain Confirmation of Enrolment and understand any attached conditions.
  6. Visa Guidance – Provide document checklists, help with immigration processes and guide you through visa submission.
  7. Pre-Departure & Arrival Support – Guidance on accommodation, living costs, work rights, insurance and arrival checklist.
