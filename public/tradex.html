Trade X — Import & Export Services

Trade X is the specialist trade arm of Himalayan White Cloud. We connect retailers, wholesalers and distributors with reliable everyday FMCG products sourced directly from trusted manufacturers in China and India. By working without unnecessary middlemen, we focus on competitive pricing, consistent quality and stable supply for fast-moving consumer goods.

Grocery & FMCG Products

Everyday food and non-food items suitable for supermarkets, minimarts and general stores. We work with high-volume producers to support repeat, pallet- or container-based orders rather than one-off samples.

Electronics & Small Appliances

Selected consumer electronics and accessories with proven demand in international markets. We prioritise models with stable supply, reliable performance and clear after-sales support arrangements.

Cleaning Materials

Household and commercial cleaning products and accessories sourced from large-scale manufacturers. Our focus is on consistent quality, safe formulations and packaging that meets your local regulations.

Direct From Manufacturers

Himalayan White Cloud works directly with vetted factories and large suppliers in China and India. This direct model helps clients avoid unreliable intermediaries and unlock:

  • Better price negotiation based on realistic volume and long-term cooperation.
  • Options for customised packing, private labels or tailored bundles for your market.
  • Improved control over production slots, quality checkpoints and shipment schedules.

End-to-End Trade Support

Product Sourcing & Quotes

We collect your target product list and markets, then source options from multiple factories. You receive comparative quotations, minimum order quantities and basic specifications so you can decide confidently.

Order Consolidation & Quality Checks

Our team coordinates order consolidation, basic quality checks at origin and packing arrangements. We work closely with freight and logistics partners so that goods move efficiently from factory to port.

Documentation & Customs Support

Trade X prepares key commercial documents such as invoices and packing lists and provides basic guidance on import/export paperwork requirements. We stay in touch throughout the shipment so you always know the next step.

Who We Work With

Trade X is designed for importers, wholesalers, retailers and regional distributors who require reliable, repeat supply rather than one-off trial orders. We focus on building long-term relationships based on:

  • Steady volumes and realistic growth plans.
  • On-time deliveries supported by clear communication.
  • Transparent expectations on quality, pricing and lead times.
